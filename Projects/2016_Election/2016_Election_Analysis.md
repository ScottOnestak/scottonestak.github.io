# Analyzing the 2016 Election
#### A Tale of Two Nations

On November 8, 2016, Americans headed to the polls to elect the next President of the United States. To anyone paying any attention to the election, almost every media outlet, polling organization, and predictive modeling forecast read the same â€“ that Hillary Clinton would easily defeat Donald Trump and become the 45th President of the United States. FiveThirtyEight predicted there was a 72% chance Clinton would win. The New York Times 85%, and the Huffington Post gave an astounding 98% likelihood that Clinton would win. However, it is now 2017, and Donald Trump is the President of the United States of America. What was it that everyone seemed to miss that led voters to elect Donald Trump?
